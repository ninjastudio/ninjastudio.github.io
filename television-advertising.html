Aston band

These are thin horizontal strips played on TV along with actual content on the bottom of the screen. Duration per exposure is 10 seconds.

L band

L Bands are L shaped ads which appear on the screen with the content / TV program for a duration of 10 seconds. Ignoring this ad is difficult.

Spot advt (FCT)

The regular types of ad that are played during the commercial break on TV program.

Headline sponsorship

Name of the sponsor is announced before the headline.

Scrolls

A short duration ad which plays itself in a marquee format and scrolls from one end of the screen to another.
